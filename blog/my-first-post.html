Why you should have a go at hosting a blog

26 September 2025

I'm writing this short test post in part to share some advice.

I've just tried going through Cloudflare for domain purchasing, and I'm now having a go with their Pages tool.

Some findings

The experience was incredibly easy and is achieveable in less than an hour.

For someone with no code experience this would still be quite a viable way to set up a website.

Their domain pricing is competitive ($7 including renewals for a .work site) and the Pages functionality is free.

As someone who is hesitant to get consumed in the Amazon ecosystem, this is a great alternative. It's nice knowing no amount of page hits is going to cause hosting costs.
